Meta Ads Starter Guide

Step-by-step — from zero to your first live campaign

1

Set up Meta Business Manager

Go to business.facebook.com → Create Account → Connect your Facebook Page and Instagram @102.lb → Add payment method.

2

Install the Meta Pixel

Business Settings → Data Sources → Pixels → Add. Copy the code and paste it before </head> in your index.html. Commit to GitHub.

3

Create your first campaign

Ads Manager → Create → Objective: Messages (WhatsApp). Name it: 102_Messages_[Month]. Budget: $5–10/day.

4

Set your audience

Location: Lebanon. Age: 22–50. Interests: small business, entrepreneurship, digital marketing, online business. Size: 500k–2M. Use Advantage+ Placements.

5

Create 3 ad variations

  • Ad 1 — Problem: "Your website isn't bringing you clients" + why + DM CTA
  • Ad 2 — Solution: "From zero to fully digital in 4 weeks" + how + DM CTA
  • Ad 3 — Social Proof: Website screenshot or result + "Your competitors are already digital"
6

Launch and wait 7 days

Publish → Do not change anything for 7 days. Meta needs time to learn. Check metrics on Day 7: CTR should be above 1%, Cost per Message below $5.

7

Scale what works

After 7 days: pause weakest ad. Increase winner's budget by 20%. Create 2 new ad variations. Repeat every 7 days.
